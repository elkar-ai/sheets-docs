## Plan Mode: Strategic Thinking

Switch to Plan Mode for complex business challenges that need strategic thinking, not just calculations.

**What Plan Mode handles:**

- **Budget planning** with multiple scenarios and constraints
- **Market analysis** considering competition and opportunities
- **Growth strategy** with resource allocation and risk assessment
- **Investment decisions** with ROI projections and alternatives

### Example: Revenue Growth Planning

**Your request:** _"Plan how to grow revenue 40% next year"_

**Agent's strategic approach:**

1. Analyzes current revenue drivers and performance
2. Models different growth scenarios (new customers vs price increases vs new products)
3. Calculates resource needs (sales team, marketing budget, product development)
4. Identifies risks and creates contingency plans
5. Builds quarterly roadmap with milestones

**Result:** Complete growth strategy with financial projections and execution plan.
