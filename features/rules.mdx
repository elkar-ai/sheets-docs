---
title: "Create Your Own Rules"
---

**Create personalized formatting rules** that match your unique workflow. Elkar learns from your preferences and applies consistent formatting across all your spreadsheets.

### Key Benefits:

- **Workflow Consistency**: Apply your company's formatting standards automatically
- **Time Savings**: Set rules once, apply everywhere
- **Project Flexibility**: Activate/deactivate rules per file or project
- **Dashboard Management**: Control all rules from your [dashboard](https://sheets-app.elkar.co/)

### Example Rule:

_"Show percentages with one decimal place, keep raw data in a dedicated 'Data' sheet, highlight inputs in blue and titles in dark green."_

### How It Works:

1. **Go to your dashboard** \>"Elkar Rules" OR **go to Excel chat** directly and click "Rule"
2. Define your formatting preferences
3. Test on a sample spreadsheet
4. Activate it or not depending on your projects
